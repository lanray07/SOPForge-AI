# SOPForge AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-22

SOPForge AI is an operations documentation app for creating SOPs, checklists, safety procedures, and training guides.

## Information Processed

The app may process information you enter, dictate, or save, including business names, process notes, checklist items, training notes, and generated documents.

## Local Storage

Saved documents are stored locally on your device using Apple's local persistence frameworks. This supports offline access to saved SOPs, checklists, and training guides.

## Speech Recognition

Voice-to-SOP uses microphone access and Apple's speech recognition services to convert dictated process notes into editable text. You can edit or delete the text before saving.

## AI Processing

Mock AI mode is enabled by default in the current app build. A secure backend AI endpoint placeholder is included for future production AI generation. If remote AI processing is enabled in a future version, text submitted for generation may be sent to the configured backend endpoint to create SOPs, checklists, or training guides.

## Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple through StoreKit. SOPForge AI does not directly collect payment card information.

## Data Sharing

SOPForge AI does not sell personal data. Documents may be shared only when you choose to export or share them using the system share sheet.

## Professional Review

AI-generated documents must be reviewed before use. SOPForge AI does not provide legal advice, regulatory certification, engineering certification, medical advice, or a replacement for qualified professionals.
