Common Questions

  • Documents are saved locally on your device for offline access.
  • AI-generated SOPs, checklists, and training guides should be reviewed before use.
  • Voice-to-SOP requires microphone and speech recognition permissions.
  • PDF exports can be shared with the native iOS share sheet.

Billing

Subscriptions are handled by Apple through StoreKit. To manage or cancel a subscription, open Settings on your device, tap your Apple Account, then tap Subscriptions.

Privacy

SOPForge AI is designed to keep saved documents on device. Read the privacy policy for more detail.

Privacy Policy

SOPForge AI documents are not legal advice, regulatory certification, engineering certification, medical advice, or a replacement for qualified professionals.
