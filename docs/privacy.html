SOPForge AI Privacy Policy

SOPForge AI helps users create SOPs, checklists, and training documents. Saved documents are stored locally on the device using Apple's local persistence frameworks.

Data Collection

SOPForge AI does not require account registration and does not collect personal data through the app.

Documents

Documents created in the app remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to export or share them.

Speech Recognition

Voice-to-SOP uses Apple's speech recognition and microphone permission flows. Users control whether these permissions are granted.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple through StoreKit. SOPForge AI does not directly collect payment card information.
